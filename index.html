Something about me

Hi! I'm a 20 year old visual design student, in LUCA Gent. Last year I also studied graphic design in Antwerp, but chose to switch schools, due preference of teaching styles.

Right now my goal is to learn as many different techniques of this industry and to not focus on one specific thing. With the purpose that I can go in different direction after my study. But if you wonder where my preference stands, it's the advertisement community. To create packaging and labels for different brands. But on the other side doing this, creating a website fascinates me aswell (I'm a beginner in the webdesign community so bare with me ;))

And if I'm not hours behind my computer I find joy in the simpel things, like hanging out with friends and spending time outside. I'm someone who likes things to be simpel yet powerfull enough to send a message, something you can find in some of my work.
